The Powwow Art Contest

Prize: $200

The Stanford Powwow Committee is soliciting entries for our art contest. The winning work will be displayed on our poster and/or t-shirt and/or publicity within the Bay Area and Northern California. We are looking for original artwork, drawings or paintings, that relate to nation-building and the spirit of powwow. We prefer your submission to be 11" by 14" or smaller.

Deadline: March 15, 2004
