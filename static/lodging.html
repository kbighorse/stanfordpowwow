Camping

The least expensive option, camping, is always available on a first-come, first-serve basis (we have lots of room!).  Campers will not be allowed into the camp site until Friday, May 7, at 5pm.  We ask for a donation per vehicle/campsite.  All campers must register on site with the Committee.  Campers are expected to sign a contract stating that the campers will abide by the rules set out by the Stanford Powwow Committee.  Not following the campground rules will be grounds for expulsion from the Powwow campgrounds.

Some of our rules:  reservations are not available; no open fires; no alcohol; clean up after yourselves; vehicles may not be driven in the campgrounds after dark; there is always a chance of rain.

Our camp area is near the Powwow arena, across Arboretum Rd.
