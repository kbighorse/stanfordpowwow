Directions

The Powwow is held in the Eucalyptus Grove on Stanford Campus, Palo Alto, CA. The Eucalyptus Grove is west of Stanford Stadium located at the corners of El Camino Real and Embarcadero Road in Palo Alto, CA.

Directions assume you are on the Peninsula in the San Francisco Bay Area.  Directions will direct you to Galvez and Campus Drive loop on campus, the general location of the Powwow, near parking.

Be wary of mapquest or Yahoo directions. They take you through campus which may be confusing. If you want to risk it:

  • Mapquest
  • YAHOO!
  • Google Maps  (Type into search bar: "Galvez St & Arboretum Rd Stanford, CA" like this)

From 101 (North and South)

Take the Embarcadero Road exit west toward Stanford. Embarcadero turns into Galvez Street at El Camino Real as you enter campus near Stanford Stadium. The Powwow will be to your right--follow the signs to parking.
 

From 280 (North and South)

Exit Page Mill Rd East toward Stanford.  Turn left at El Camino Real.  Turn Left at Galvez/Embarcadero near Stanford Stadium. Follow the signs to parking.
