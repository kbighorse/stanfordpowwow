Sponsors

There are many opportunities to help the Stanford Powwow. If you would like to be a sponsor for the event, please e-mail the powwow committee for sponsorship packages. Also, there are opportunities to purchase ads in the souvenir powwow program. With over 30,000 visitors, the Stanford Powwow reaches a wide audience from all walks of life. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to ask the powwow committee.
